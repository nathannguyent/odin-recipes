Shin Ramen Instant noodles

Probably one of the best tasting instant noodles you can buy, you cannot change my mind.

Ingredients

  • Shin ramen instant noodle package.

Steps

  1. Boiling water. Boil 550 mL (about 2 1/3 cups) of water. Add noodles, soup base, and vegetable mix.
  2. Cooking noodles. Cook for 4-5 minutes, stirring occasionally
  3. Enjoy. Remove from heat and serve
