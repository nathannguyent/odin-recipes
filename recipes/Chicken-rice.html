Chicken and Rice

Unique to fresh college students, the college special is an exquisite meal of unseasoned chicken, rice and in this case mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Chicken Breast
  • Sauce of your choice
    1. Ketchup
    2. Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Cook rice following these instructions, or via package instructions.
  2. As the rice is cooking, place the chicken breast into a pot of water and bring it to a boil. When the water comes to a boil, lower the temperature and bring the water to a simmer. Cook the chicken until internal temperature reaches 75°C/165°F or for roughly 12-15 minutes.
  3. Plate your dish, put as much rice on your plate as you want and as much chicken as you want.
  4. Top it off with the sauce of your choice.
