Burger

Definitely not a Mcdonalds burger and is homemade. The cheapest and easiest burger ever!

Ingredients

  • About 2$

Steps

  1. Go to your local Mcdonalds by any means possible.
  2. Approach either the cashregister or the kiosks.
  3. If approaching cash register, turn around and go to the kiosk, the anxiety is not worth it. If approaching the kiosk, look for the item named "cheese burger" and add to order.
  4. Proceed to checkout and get the recipet.
  5. Go to the waiting area to pick up your burger.
  6. ENJOY!
